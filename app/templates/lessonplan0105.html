Relax, Reboot, Reimagine: Visioning and planning the tools we'll build in this program

Lesson Plan - Week 1, Day {{shortcode}}

Projected date: Friday April 14, 2014
Instructor(s): Fox, Rahman, Wiggins



Learning outcomes

After this lesson, participants will be able to:

  • Plan and record a reflection video, photo set, and/or blog
  • Explain the underlying values of the human-centered design process
  • Explain the concept of "socially responsible design"
  • Write a no-code test for test-driven development of a simple everyday machine
  • Explain the program's two toolkits and community project to someone who is not here

Prep required before start of lesson

  • Test video setup and YouTube/Vimeo/Vine/Instagram/whatever channel

Materials to bring (instructional team)

Note that whiteboards, easel pads, markers, projector, and screen should be in place for all lessons

  • No special materials for this lesson.

Materials to bring (participants)

Note that participants should bring their computers, USB drives, notebooks, and course text every day

  • Show-and-tell object/photo
