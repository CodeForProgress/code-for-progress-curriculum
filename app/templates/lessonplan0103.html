Master's Tools, Remastered Tools, Native Tools: Critical-conceptual app design using things we already know

Lesson Plan - Week 1, Day {{shortcode}}

Projected date: Wednesday April 16, 2014
Instructor(s): Fox, Rahman, Wiggins



Learning outcomes

After this lesson, participants will be able to:

  • Identify what we’ve been told about coders/programmers/app developers, who they are, what they look like, and why this is the case
  • Identify some of the current roles of developers in the social justice movement
  • Distinguish between “technology,” “code,” and “applications”
  • Identify three objects we see or use daily that rely on code to run
  • Explain the difference between tools (objects produced) and “technologies” (ways of being in the world made possible by those tools) for common products in our lives
  • Discuss how specific digital technologies build power, and who benefits from this
